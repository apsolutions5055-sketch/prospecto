FAQ

Questions we get
all the time.

Do I need to do anything daily to use Prospecto? ↓
Nothing. You set your ICP once — takes 5 minutes. After that, Prospecto runs every night automatically and your leads are waiting in your inbox at 8am. Zero daily effort required.
How is this different from Apollo.io? ↓
Apollo is a database — you still have to search manually every day, filter, research each company, figure out the pain point, and write your own outreach. Prospecto does all of that automatically overnight. Apollo gives you a fishing lake. Prospecto delivers fresh fish every morning.
Where does the lead data come from? ↓
We source leads from a database of 275M+ companies and contacts. Our AI then cross-references public signals — job postings, funding rounds, news, tech stack, reviews — to score and research each lead before it reaches you.
How accurate is the AI scoring? ↓
Our AI scores leads 1–10 based on how closely they match your ICP and how many buying signals they show. It's not perfect — no AI is. But it's consistently better than manual research because it processes dozens of signals simultaneously that a human would take hours to check.
What is the Sales Vault? ↓
The Sales Vault is a set of 4 battle-tested sales documents built from 10 years of studying the world's top closers — Belfort, Cardone, Hormozi, Miner, Tracy, Hopkins, Ziglar, Kennedy, Brunson and Blount. It includes a cold calling script, objection handlers, pre-call blueprint, and follow-up sequences. Every subscriber gets full access.
What happens after the 7-day free trial? ↓
After 7 days you're automatically moved to your selected plan and billed monthly. You can cancel anytime before the trial ends and you'll never be charged. No hidden fees, no contracts.
Is the founder pricing really locked forever? ↓
Yes. The first 100 founding members keep their price for life — even when we raise prices for new users. As long as your subscription stays active, your rate never changes. This is our commitment to early believers.
Can I cancel anytime? ↓
Absolutely. Cancel from your account settings in 30 seconds. No calls, no forms, no friction. You keep access until the end of your billing period.
